## Observability and authentication

No vendor SDK is mandated. Evaluate the chosen auth/crash/telemetry package with the same rubric, including native platform support, privacy, symbol upload, offline buffering, sampling/cardinality, binary/startup cost, license/service terms, and whether backend/platform capabilities suffice.
